I am an NSF graduate research fellow, starting my second year of PhD studies in the computer science department at Johns Hopkins University. In addition to the department, I am part of the Center for Language and Speech Processing and the Institute for Computational Medicine.

Bien que mes recherches ne se concentrent plus sur le traitement automatique du langage naturel, je suis toujours passionnée par les langues. 我希望用语言识别的办法为了提高医生研究。毕竟语音和心率都是时间序列。Baina oro har, niri atzerritar hizkuntzak gustatzen zaizkit. 기회가 외국어를 말하는 것 항상 노린다.
